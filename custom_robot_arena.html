Review : Custom Robo Arena DS!

Posted on February 10, 2026 by Hugues Astier
Retro Gaming Solo Game

Presentation

This game is edited and developed by Nintendo, it was released the may 25th 2007, its genre is an rpg,adventure and fighting game also, this game is on the nintendo lite or nintendo 3ds.

The Story

Regarding the story, you will play as a high school student, who recently move in the new city because his father is a searcher in a biggest laboratory of game. Already, we can reconize the idea of a challenger, it mean that in this game we will have to make new friends, new activities, clearly a new life. So almost immediatly in the game we understand that the world is turning around robot, indeed you will receive a robot, and a lot of people you will meet have one. This robots are using essentially to make fight in the game, but the story explain that not only, it's used in the society in general like for the war or medicine. So what is exciting in this game is to see its own robot improving thanks to the purchase of new arms or by cleaning it (yes, you will have to do it otherwise you robot loose its perfomance) and to see your character becoming an important person in this world by winning tournanments

Technical aspect and Gameplay

2D vs 3D views

About one of the fundamental aspect of the video games, we can say that the mix between 3d and 2d is not shocking at all, you have a good transition between the both, the 2d is for the adventure and the 3d for the fight (see the pictures beside). Beside that, the fights are really enjoyable, they are really dynamics and full of tension.

The Verdict

For all the reasons mentioned above, I really encourage every teenager or nostalgic player to play this game, or to discover this fun and cool story for the first time.
